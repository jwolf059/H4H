Thurston County Coordinated Entry

Over the past 3 years County staff and dedicated service providers have worked diligently to create a Coordinated Entry system that quickly evaluates the needs of a client and connects them directly to the best possible housing solution. Click for more

Landlord Liason Program

For years there has been an ever increasing need for a unbiased voice in the landlord - ternate relationship. The landlord liaison program aims to mitigate conflicts between low-income renters and their landlords with the hope to lower the chance of evictions.

Community Investment Partnership

The CIP (Community Investment Partnership) is the decision making body for HOME, 2163, 2060, CHG, ESG, and HHSC funding awards in Thurston county. Projects span from Social Services programs to the construction of affordable housing.
