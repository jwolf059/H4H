Housing Resources

Shelters

Shelters in Thurston County are designed to be a temporary solution for Community members who are homeless. Homeless indviduals will stay in the Shelters until a permante housing solution is found. Thurston County currenlty have shelters that provide services for three sub-demographic groups: Families, Transitional Youth, and single indviduals.

  • Salvation Army (Adults without Children)
  • Interfaith Works (Single Adults)
  • Young Adult Shelter (Youth 18-24)
  • Yelm Community Services
  • Gospel Mission

Domestic Violence Shelter

Provided Shelter and supportive services to domestic violence victims and their children. All domestic violence shelters are secure on confidential ensuring the safty of the client at all times.

  • Partners in Prevention Education (Emergency Hotel Vouchers)
  • Safeplace
  • Turning Point

Homeless Housing

Homeless Housing program are designed for Homeless community members who require permanate supportive service in order to be house permanetly.

  • Arbor Manor Transitional Housing
  • Drexel House Transitional Housing
  • Fleetwood Permanent Supportive Housing
  • Quixote Village
